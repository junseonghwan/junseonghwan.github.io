Research Interests

At a high level, I am interested in computational statistics and Bayesian statistics with applications to single cell data for cancer phylogenetics. Some key words related to my research interests:

  • Sequential Monte Carlo methods
  • Probabilistic analysis of single-cell RNA and multi-modal data
  • Bayesian inference for cancer phylogenetics
  • Statistical inference for combinatorial random variables
  • Probabilistic programming
