About Research

Research Interests

At a high level, I am interested in computational statistics and Bayesian learning.

  • Sequential Monte Carlo methods
  • Bayesian nonparametrics
  • Distributed algorithms for large scale inference problems
  • Latent variable models
  • Probabilistic programming
  • Statistical inference for combinatorial random variables
  • Sports analytics
